Jatin Batra

About Me

I am a faculty member at the School of Technology and Computer Science (STCS), TIFR Mumbai. My current research interests lie in theoretical machine learning, especially in non-convex optimization, high-dimensional probability, and applications of AI to the natural sciences. Previously, I worked on approximation algorithms and scheduling.
